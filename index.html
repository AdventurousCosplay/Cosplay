How this app works?

You can create a plan, where you can mark down your budget, add pictures of the costume you are making and section the costume into parts.
Then you can mark down what you need for that costume and the cost of it.

You can also gain levels by making costumes and shareing their drafts if you are a premium account. After making and wearing the costume
you can post pictures of the results and share your experiences with other users or just your friends over the private messages.

Once you have made the list you can tick the things you have already bought.

Who is this app for?

Anyone who wishes to make costumes, props or any other crafts can use this app and share their ideas.

App community

One of the prime features of _Adventurous_Cosplay_ that is included in the premium version, is that you can share the drafts
you make with other people. That way users who don't gave a lot of time or don't know how to plan can find a start somewhere.
But no matter who you are you still have access to sharing your photos and use drafts that others have made.

Conventions

To support the production of the app, there will be held at least 2 conventions every year, where cosplayers of all genres cn come together and have a good time.
There will be lots of activities, merch and much much more.
You can also meet all of the friends you made within the app there and of course make new ones.
And of course the people who gave their all and helped out a lot of the beginner cosplayers,
the ones with the highest levels and the highest amount of costumes, will be rewarded for their hard work.
